About Us

Nuts and Bolts is a family run hardware store that has been selling the highest quality products at the lowest prices for over 50 years!
Founded in 1955, Nuts and Bolts continues to provide a great selection of products to customers. With 10 locations to pick from, we have central Ohio's hardware needs covered!
